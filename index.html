Google Fu moves:
  • Exact phrase: "use quotes around"
  • All of the words: just plain words
  • One of the words: this OR that OR word
  • Without the words: -not -this -words
  • Numbers range: 10..20 kg 2011..2014
  • Only selected page: site:example.com
  • Older than selected date: before:YYYY-MM-DD
  • Newer than selected date: after:YYYY-MM-DD

Of course you can merge any of these moves to create a combo :)

Search for the Ada language or its subset SPARK related things on the Web. This search engine is powered by Google Custom Search engine. After search, you can sort your results by relevance or date. The search engine is customized to find only Ada/SPARK related things, thus queries like "gps" return only "GNAT Programming Studio" related pages.
